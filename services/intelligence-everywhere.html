The Challenge

Legacy manufacturing site struggled with fragmented protocols and data silos across production zones.

The Breakdown:

  • • Factory sensors ran on LoRaWAN, while legacy controllers used Modbus and CAN Bus
  • • Vendor-specific hubs created data silos across production zones
  • • MQTT brokers were misaligned, causing telemetry drift and blind spots
  • • No unified dashboard—operators relied on manual reconciliation and guesswork
  • • Tariff shocks and supply chain volatility demanded rapid production pivots—without the infrastructure to support them

The Rock8 Approach

Unified fragmented protocols into intelligent, orchestrated infrastructure with real-time visibility.

Our Solution Architecture:

  • • Partnered with client engineers and protocol specialists to map all topologies—LoRaWAN, MQTT, OPC UA, Modbus, TCP/IP, CAN Bus
  • • Deployed modular adapters to normalize telemetry across microcontrollers and hubs
  • • Built a resilient orchestration layer with rollback logic and sentinel detection
  • • Enabled real-time dashboards with unified visibility from edge to cloud—empowering rapid capacity shifts and strategic decision-making
  • • Accelerated time-to-value with sub-week rollout across production zones—driving client retention and competitive wins
