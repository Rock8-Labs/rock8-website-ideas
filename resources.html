AI & Automation Trends: 2024 Insights & 2025 Outlook

This comprehensive study by AIIM (Association for Intelligent Information Management) was conducted to assess the current state of AI and automation adoption across global organizations in 2024. The research involved surveying over 1,200 business leaders and IT professionals across various industries to understand automation maturity levels, implementation challenges, and future outlook. The study aimed to identify gaps between digital transformation aspirations and actual achievements, particularly focusing on process automation and AI integration.

Key Findings & Statistics

  • 45% of business processes remain paper-based - indicating massive digitization opportunities
  • Only 3% of organizations have achieved advanced automation with AI/ML technologies
  • 67% of companies struggle with process identification for automation candidates
  • 52% cite lack of technical expertise as the primary barrier to AI implementation
  • 78% plan to increase automation investments in 2025 despite current challenges
  • Average ROI of 312% reported by organizations with successful automation implementations
  • 89% of manual processes could be partially automated using current technology
  • Organizations with advanced automation see 40% faster decision-making and 35% cost reduction
